Carnegie Mellon University

All University Orchestra

The AUO Board and Music Director are in the process of developing ways students can continue pursuing their interests in classical music outside their studies through this organization. Ideas include creating virtual chamber ensembles, smaller in-person chamber groups (TBD), and/or inviting guest speakers to discuss topics about conductors, minority composers, etc. Chamber Ensembles, String Theory and Flute Choir, will not be meeting during the fall semester. At this time we are not taking new members for in-person rehearsals/concerts for the fall 2020 semester. However, you are still welcome to join our online discussions and virtual ensembles if we decide to pursue this option. If you have questions regarding the AUO's plans for the upcoming fall semester and/or are interested in joining, please contact: auo-board@lists.andrew.cmu.edu.
