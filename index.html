Welcome

The Carnegie Mellon All University Orchestra is an ensemble composed of CMU undergrads, graduate students, staff, and faculty who love to make music. See the Auditions page for audition materials. Everyone is welcome!

The AUO puts on one concert per semester, available free of charge to members of the CMU community and the public. See our Concerts page for this year's schedule and recordings of some of our past concerts.
