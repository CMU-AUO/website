About AUO

The All University Orchestra was founded in 1997 by David Koes, with the aim of giving any student at Carnegie Mellon the opportunity to perform with a symphony orchestra.

We hold a concert each semester. Rehearsals usually take place on Sunday evenings, in the Conan Room at the University Center; details of the current schedule are here.

If you are interested in joining AUO, just come along to a rehearsal at the start of a semester, or email us first if you have any questions.

If you would like to help AUO keep putting on free concerts, you can now donate online - under 'designation', select 'other', and specify 'All University Orchestra' in the comments box.

For more about AUO, read our Constitution or our History
