AUO Schedule

AUO meets on Sunday nights from 7:30pm to 10:00pm in the UC Conan room, with sectionals or social events often happening at 6:30pm. You can download the 2013-2014 "syllabus" with our complete schedule and other resources here.

AUO's Fall 2013 Concert will be Sunday, November 24. The venue and time will be announced here as the concert approaches. As always, AUO Concerts are free and open to the public.

The Orchestra's repertoire for the Fall 2013 semester will consist of Brahms "Tragic Overture", Mozart "Symphony No. 25 in G Minor", Dvorak "Symphony No. 8 in G Major," and an additional piece TBA.

String Theory's repertoire for the Fall 2013 semester is TBA.
