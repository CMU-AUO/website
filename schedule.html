AUO Schedule

AUO meets on Sunday nights from 7:30pm to 10:00pm in the UC Conan room. Our first rehearsal for the Fall 2013 semester will be on September 8. Check back here for our full schedule once we start rehearsals.

The Orchestra's repertoire for the Fall 2013 semester will consist of Brahms "Tragic Overture", Mozart "Symphony No. 25 in G Minor", Dvorak "Symphony No. 8 in G Major," and an additional piece TBA.
