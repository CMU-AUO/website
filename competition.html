2014 Concerto & Aria Competition

We're excited to announce our first ever Concerto & Aria Competition for 2014. The competition will be held on April 19, 2014, and winners may be selected to perform with the AUO in the 2014-2015 concert season. Applications are due by Sunday, February 23. If you have any questions, please email competition@alluniversityorchestra.org. The application form can be found here.
