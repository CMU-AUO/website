Welcome

Welcome to the website of Carnegie Mellon's All University Orchestra. The AUO is an organization composed of CMU undergrads, graduate students, staff, and faculty who love to make music. Everyone is welcome to participate! Please see the Auditions page for audition materials.

The AUO puts on one concert per semester, available free of charge to members of the CMU community and the public. See our Concerts page for this year's schedule and recordings of some of our past concerts.

Here's What's New

Former AUO Music Director Leads Upcoming Performing Arts Company

September 13, 2014

Former AUO Music Director, Maria Sensi Sellner (2002 – 2012) is artistic director of a new and vibrant performing arts company, Resonance Works | Pittsburgh.

On September 19th and 21st, the company will be presenting Donizetti ºs Elixir of Love in the George R. White Studio at the Pittsburgh Opera located in the Strip District. This is not a traditional opera experience but rather a cabaret, in which the audience is part of the performance.

For more information about the performance and other Resonance Works projects, please visit www.resonanceworks.org .

Welcome Back

Hi, everybody!

I hope you all had amazing summers filled with interesting adventures. Now that school is back is session, we’re going to start up rehearsals again!
