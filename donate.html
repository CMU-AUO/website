Support AUO

AUO can't survive without your help! We want to be able to provide free, awesome concerts to our community and a place for our members to play for years to come, but we can't without you. To make a donation to AUO, follow these easy steps:

Go to CMU's donate online page and fill in your information.

Under designation, mark "other" and type "All University Orchestra" into the box.

Fill in your payment information. Thank you for supporting AUO!

If you have questions, you can contact us at auo@andrew.cmu.edu .
