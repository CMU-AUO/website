Those interested in playing with the AUO Symphony are required to audition for seating placement. Returning AUO Symphony members do not need to audition, and those interested in playing with String Theory or Flutonium do not need to audition. Auditions are sent using a video link (YouTube, Vimeo,etc) to AUO Music Director, Jeffrey Klefstad -- cmuauo@gmail.com. Click the link to your respective instrument and follow the directions provided.

Deadline is Friday, August 31st at 10:00pm EST.

At this point, we are not taking auditions for Piano, or non-traditional orchestral instruments. All Percussionists will have a short audition during the first rehearsal to assign parts.
