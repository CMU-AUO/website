Spring 2022 Audition Materials



New members interested in joining the AUO Symphony should use the audition material by clicking the "Download Audition Materials" button below, choosing their respective instruments, and following the directions indicated in the audition packet. Materials should be submitted by January 1st, 2022--10:00pm.


Violin 1 Violin 2 Viola Cello


Those interested in playing with the AUO Symphony are required to audition for seating placement. Returning AUO Symphony members do not need to audition, and those interested in playing with the AUO Chamber Orchestra or Flutonium do not need to audition. Auditions are sent using a video link (YouTube, Vimeo,etc) to AUO Music Director, Dr. Jeffrey Klefstad -- cmuauo@gmail.com. Click the "Download Audition Materials" button above, select your respective instrument, and follow the directions provided.

Deadline is Saturday, January 1st - 10:00pm.

*"We are unable to take new woodwind, trumpet, horn, or tuba players at this time. We do have openings for trombone. Please contact AUO Music Director Dr. Klefstad for any questions.
