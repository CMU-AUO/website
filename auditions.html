Spring 2024 Audition Materials



New members interested in joining the AUO Symphony should use the audition materials by clicking the "Download Audition Materials" button below, choosing their respective instruments, and following the directions indicated in the audition packet. Materials should be submitted by Friday, January 5, 2024 - 11:59 PM.


Violin 1 Violin 2 Viola Cello Bass Flute Oboe Clarinet Bassoon Horn Trumpet Trombone Tuba


Those interested in playing with the AUO Symphony are required to audition for seating placement. Returning AUO Symphony members do not need to audition, and those interested in playing with the AUO Chamber Orchestra or Flutonium do not need to audition. Auditions are sent using a video link (YouTube, Vimeo,etc) to AUO Music Director, Dr. Jeffrey Klefstad -- cmuauo@gmail.com. Click the "Download Audition Materials" button above, select your respective instrument, and follow the directions provided.

Deadline is Friday, January 5, 2024 - 11:59 PM.

*To all students interested in auditioning for the AUO, please note that we are limited to how many woodwinds we can accommodate for the AUO Symphony Orchestra (e.g., flute and clarinet). Those interested in percussion will be auditioned during rehearsal. Cello and bass players, you are asked to bring your own instruments or pursue renting one before arriving on campus. You are asked to store your own instruments as we do not have the space to store them ourselves.
