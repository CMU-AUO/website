Fall 2021 Audition Materials

New members interested in joining the AUO Symphony should use the audition material by clicking the link for their instrument below and follow the directions indicated in the audition packet. Materials should be submitted by August 28, 2021--10:00pm. If planning to join during the spring semester, please submit your audition by January 1st, 2022.

  • Violin 1
  • Violin 2
  • Viola
  • Cello
  • Bass
  • Flute
  • Oboe/English Horn
  • Clarinet
  • Bassoon
  • Horn
  • Trumpet
  • Trombone

Those interested in playing with the AUO Symphony are required to audition for seating placement. Returning AUO Symphony members do not need to audition, and those interested in playing with String Theory or Flutonium do not need to audition. Auditions are sent using a video link (YouTube, Vimeo,etc) to AUO Music Director, Dr. Jeffrey Klefstad -- cmuauo@gmail.com. Click the link to your respective instrument and follow the directions provided.

Deadline is Saturday, August 28th - 10:00pm.

At this point, we are not taking auditions for Piano, or non-traditional orchestral instruments. All Percussionists will have a short audition during the first rehearsal to assign parts.
