AUO Symphony

AUO Symphony meets every Sunday from 6:00 - 8:30pm in the Studio Theater located in the University Center. Sectionals are held after rehearsals and are announced the week leading up to rehearsal.

Click Here for the PDF version of the Fall 2019 AUO Schedule

Please note the current schedule is not fully detailed and is subject to change. Members are expected to read the weekly email sent out by the Music Director for any changes. Sectional Coachings will be announced in rehearsals at latest the week prior to coaching.

String Theory and Flutonium

String Theory meets every Saturday afternoon from 4:30 - 6:00pm in Margaret Morrison 119. Flutonium meets from 6:00 - 7:00pm in Margaret Morrison 119.
