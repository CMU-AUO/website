Rehearsal Calendar

AUO Symphony

The AUO Symphony meets every Sunday from 6:00 - 8:30pm in the Studio Theater located in the Cohen University Center. Sectionals are held after rehearsals and are announced the week leading up to rehearsal.

AUO Chamber Orchestra

The AUO Chamber Orchestra meets every Sunday in the CUC Studio Theater between 8:30-10:00pm.
