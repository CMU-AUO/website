title: Former AUO Music Director Leads Upcoming Performing Arts Company
---
<div class="row">

    <div class="large-8 columns">
        <p>
        Former AUO Music Director, Maria Sensi Sellner (2002 - 2012) is artistic director of a new and 
        vibrant performing arts company, Resonance Works | Pittsburgh.
        </p>
        <p>
        On September 19th and 21st, the company will be presenting Donizetti ºs <i>Elixir of Love</i> in
        the George R. White  Studio at the Pittsburgh Opera located in the Strip District. This is not 
        a traditional opera experience but rather a cabaret, in which the audience is part of the 
        performance.
        </p>
        <p>
        For more information about the performance and other Resonance Works projects, please visit 
        "www.resonanceworks.org":http://www.resonanceworks.org .
        </p>
