Support Us!



The AUO cannot survive without your help! We want to continue providing free, enriching concerts for our community and a place for our members to play for years to come, but we cannot without your support. To make a donation to the AUO, follow these easy steps:

  1. Go to CMU's donate online page and fill in your information.
  2. Under designation, mark "other" and type "All University Orchestra" into the box.
  3. Fill in your payment information. Thank you for supporting AUO!

If you have questions, you can ask us after joining our Discord!
