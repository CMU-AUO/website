The AUO cannot survive without your help! We want to continue providing free, enriching concerts for our community and a place for our members to play for years to come, but we cannot without your support. To make a donation to the AUO, follow these easy steps:

- Go to CMU's donate online page and fill in your information.

- Under designation, mark "other" and type "All University Orchestra" into the box.

- Fill in your payment information. Thank you for supporting AUO!

If you have questions, you can contact us at auo-board@lists.andrew.cmu.edu .
